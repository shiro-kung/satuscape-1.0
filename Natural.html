Natural Attractions

Explore Natural Attractions

Koh Lipe

Best Time to Visit: November to April (calm seas and clear water)

What to Know:

  • Famous for snorkeling, diving, and vibrant coral reefs.
  • Accessible by speedboat from Pak Bara Pier.
  • Entry to Tarutao National Park requires a fee (approx. 200 THB for foreigners).

Prasat Hin Panyod

Best Time to Visit: November to April (to enjoy emerald-green water and pleasant weather)

What to Know:

  • A unique limestone formation resembling a castle.
  • Accessible by local longtail boats.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking on rugged terrain.

Dragon Spine Beach (Hat San Lang Mangkon)

Best Time to Visit: During low tide (consult locals for timing)

What to Know:

  • A rare natural phenomenon where a sandbar emerges, allowing you to walk in the middle of the sea.

Koh Hin Ngam

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Known for its glossy black pebbles.
  • It’s forbidden to remove any stones as souvenirs.

Koh Hin Son

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Features unique balanced rock formations that defy gravity.

Koh Adang

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Ideal for hiking, snorkeling, and beach activities.
  • Offers panoramic views of Koh Lipe from its viewpoint.

Koh Bulon

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • A tranquil island perfect for diving and snorkeling.
  • Limited accommodation, so book early.

Koh Khai

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Famous for its natural stone arch, a popular photo spot.
  • Symbolic for lovers, as legend says couples who pass through the arch will have eternal love.

Pak Bara Beach

Best Time to Visit: Year-round

What to Know:

  • The main gateway to Satun’s islands.
  • Offers fresh seafood restaurants and peaceful seaside views.

Phu Pha Phet Cave

Best Time to Visit: Year-round

What to Know:

  • One of the largest caves in Thailand, featuring stalactites and stalagmites.
  • Proper footwear and a guide are recommended.

Tham Le Stegodon

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Thailand's longest water cave, rich in fossils.
  • A guided boat tour is required to explore the cave.

Thale Ban National Park

Best Time to Visit: Year-round

What to Know:

  • Features lush mountains, large wetlands, and rare wildlife like serows.
  • Entry fee is required (approx. 200 THB for foreigners).

Tham Chet Kot (Seven Bends Cave)

Best Time to Visit: Year-round

What to Know:

  • A winding cave with stunning rock formations.
  • Requires basic fitness for exploration.

Wang Sai Thong Waterfall

Best Time to Visit: July to October (rainy season for golden water cascades)

What to Know:

  • Features unique limestone formations with golden reflections.

Koh Rawi

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • A peaceful island with white sand beaches and clear waters.

Sunrise Beach

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Famous for spectacular sunrises and serenity.

Koh Kra

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Offers stunning 360-degree views and pristine beaches.

Jabang Channel

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Renowned for vibrant coral reefs.
  • Snorkeling equipment is available for rent on-site.

Tharn Pliew Waterfall

Best Time to Visit: July to October

What to Know:

  • A small yet scenic waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

Sunset Beach

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Offers stunning views of the sunset over the Andaman Sea.

Koh Yang

Best Time to Visit: November to April

What to Know:

  • Known for coral reefs and turtle nesting sites.
